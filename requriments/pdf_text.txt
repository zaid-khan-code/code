Helplytics AI – Community Support Platform
🌍 1. Problem Statement
In today’s learning ecosystem:
Students struggle to find timely help
Skilled individuals are willing to help but lack a platform
No structured system exists for:
Asking for help
Offering help
Tracking contributions
Result:
Slower learning
Missed opportunities
Weak community collaboration
💡 2. Vision
Build a multi-page, AI-powered community platform that connects people who need help with those who
can provide it.
